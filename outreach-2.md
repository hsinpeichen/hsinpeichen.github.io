<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Astronomy Club Union of Universities in Taiwan (2020-2024)</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>During 2020-2022, I served as one of the directors at the <a href="https://astrounion.org/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Astronomy Club Union of Universities in Taiwan (ACUUT)</a>, an organization dedicated to supporting university astronomy clubs in Taiwan. Especially, during the solar eclipse in June 2020, I coordinated the national live-streaming program of ACUUT in collaboration with the Central Weather Bureau in Taiwan, ultimately attracting around 290,000 viewers. Through these experiences, I have acquired valuable skills in project organization, team leadership, and effective collaboration.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Astronomy for the Public in Austin (2024-)</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>As an international student who left my home country at the age of 26, I am currently learning to engage with the local community in Austin, TX by regularly attending <a href="https://astronomyontap.org/locations/austin-tx/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Astronomy on Tap ATX</a> events. My goal is to give at least one public talk before I graduate in 2030, hopefully more!</p>
